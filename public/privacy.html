隐私政策 · 思考记录

最后更新:2026 年 6 月 16 日

「思考记录」是一款帮助你用语音记录每天的思考、并由 AI 跨多天提炼你反复在想的主题的 app。我们尽量少碰你的数据。本政策说明这款 app 收集什么、怎么用、存在哪。

1. 我们处理的数据

  • 你记录的文字:你录入或输入的想法内容。
  • 麦克风:仅在你主动点录音时使用,用于把语音转成文字。我们不保存音频文件。

这款 app 没有账号系统,不收集你的姓名、手机号、位置或设备标识用于追踪。

2. 你的数据存在哪

你的所有记录默认只存在你自己的设备上(浏览器/app 的本地存储)。我们没有保存你记录内容的服务器数据库。卸载 app 或在 app 内删除记录,数据即从设备移除。

3. 涉及的第三方服务

  • 语音转文字:在 iOS app 中使用 iOS 系统自带的语音识别功能,这可能会将你的语音音频发送给 Apple 进行转写(详见 Apple 的隐私政策)。在网页版中使用浏览器内置的语音识别服务。
  • 主题分析(AI):当你查看「主题」时,你的文字记录会通过我们的中转服务发送给 Groq(groq.com)的大语言模型做分析,并把结果返回给你。我们的中转服务只做转发、不存储你的内容。请参考 Groq 的隐私政策了解其数据处理方式。

4. 我们不做的事

  • 不展示广告。
  • 不接入第三方统计/追踪 SDK。
  • 不出售或出租你的数据。

5. 删除你的数据

在 app 内删除对应记录,或卸载 app,即可删除本机数据。由于内容只存在你的设备上,删除后我们无从恢复。

6. 儿童

本 app 不面向 13 岁以下儿童,也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 政策变更

如有更新,我们会修改本页面并更新顶部的日期。

Privacy Policy · Thought Journal

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Thought Journal is an app that lets you record your daily thoughts by voice and uses AI to surface the themes you keep thinking about across days. We handle as little of your data as possible. This policy explains what the app collects, how it is used, and where it is stored.

1. Data we process

  • The text you record: the thoughts you dictate or type.
  • Microphone: used only when you tap record, to convert speech to text. We do not save audio files.

The app has no accounts and does not collect your name, phone number, location, or device identifiers for tracking.

2. Where your data is stored

By default, all of your entries are stored only on your own device (the browser/app local storage). We do not run a server database that holds your entries. Deleting entries in the app, or uninstalling the app, removes the data from your device.

3. Third-party services

  • Speech-to-text: in the iOS app we use Apple's built-in speech recognition, which may send your voice audio to Apple for transcription (see Apple's privacy policy). On the web we use the browser's built-in speech recognition service.
  • Theme analysis (AI): when you open "Themes", your text entries are sent through our relay to Groq (groq.com) language models for analysis, and the result is returned to you. Our relay only forwards the request and does not store your content. See Groq's privacy policy for how they handle data.

4. What we do not do

  • No advertising.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or rent your data.

5. Deleting your data

Delete the relevant entries in the app, or uninstall the app, to remove the on-device data. Because content lives only on your device, we cannot recover it once deleted.

6. Children

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make changes, we will update this page and the date at the top.
