常见问题

  • 怎么记录? 在「记录」页点圆形按钮开始说话,文字会自动出现,也可以手动改,然后保存。
  • 我的记录存在哪? 默认只存在你自己的设备上。详见隐私政策。
  • 「主题」是怎么来的? 由 AI 跨多天分析你的记录得出,只看最近的记录。

Muse · Support

Muse is an app for recording your daily thoughts by voice. You speak, it turns your words into text, and AI helps surface the themes you keep returning to and how your views change over time.

FAQ

  • How do I record? On the Record tab, tap the round button and start speaking. The text appears automatically; you can edit it, then save.
  • Where are my entries stored? By default, only on your own device. See the privacy policy.
  • How are "Themes" generated? By AI analyzing your recent entries across days.
